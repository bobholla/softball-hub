NCAA / NAIA / JUCO Contact Rules — Key Dates for Charlie (Class of 2031)

Every division has DIFFERENT rules. D1 softball is one of the most restrictive — coaches can't initiate contact until September 1 of junior year. But D2, D3, NAIA, and JUCO coaches can reach out MUCH earlier. This means Charlie could be hearing from non-D1 programs well before D1 schools can legally call. YOU can contact coaches at ANY division at ANY time — the restrictions are only on coaches initiating.
